Easily help out updating our records...

We rely on you to help update our data! You can either update individual, or let your browser click through the FracFocusData.org website and update records automatically!

You can help out adding records to our database and get better data yourself at the same time! Install our browser extension, and the FracFocus website is ameded with links to our full site records for each permit, as well as scross-links to other sites operated by the same operator, using the same chemicals etc, plus the ability to download this data in CSV-format for viewing in Excell or similar programs!

To help out adding records to our database, first install Greasmonkey (if you have FireFox) or Tampermonkey (if you have Chrome), then

install the FracBot userscript

Finally, visit the FracFocus website and search for some sites. Remember to click "Update" on any records that are flagged as missing in our database!
